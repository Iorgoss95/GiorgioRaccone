Wellcome

I'm 27 and am an Electrical and Computer Engineer, passionate about web development and interested in building beautiful, functional design-oriented UI using modern tools.

This site is completely designed by me.
I made it in my spare time just for fun. it's just the first version, or maybe a beta version. there will be others in the future according to my personal taste in design and emotions. I made it fully responsive using flexbox, grid and media queries.
Try to change browser size and see what happens!
I also made a custom scrollbar/indicator that increase it's size in relationn of the percentage of the scrolled page (Desktop only). I used a little bit of javascript to achieve this.
As you see, another use of JS is to change theme color of the entire site! Try again and pick your favorite!
I put in some hover, scroll and some other CSS effects to show off what I like do.
